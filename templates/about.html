About LexShield

LexShield was founded with a vision to revolutionize the way legal documents are analyzed and understood. Recognizing the growing complexity and volume of contracts in both corporate and legal sectors, we set out to build a powerful AI-driven platform that simplifies legal workflows. Over time, LexShield has evolved into a robust solution that leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP), anomaly detection, and advanced entity recognition to extract key information, summarize clauses, and detect potential fraud in contracts—securely and at scale.

Why LexShield

In today’s fast-paced legal and business environments, traditional contract analysis is not only time-consuming but also vulnerable to human oversight. LexShield bridges this gap by combining advanced AI with legal intelligence to deliver instant, reliable, and secure contract analysis.With features like:
  • Automated clause summarization
  • Fraudulent clause detection through anomaly analysis
  • Key entity extraction using NER
  • Multilingual document support
  • Secure user access with encryption & optional blockchain verification
LexShield ensures that you never miss a critical detail. Whether you're a law firm, corporate legal department, or an independent professional, LexShield enhances your workflow by reducing manual effort, mitigating legal risks, and ensuring compliance — all in a streamlined, easy-to-use platform.
